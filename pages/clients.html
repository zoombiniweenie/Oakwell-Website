Compliance & Protection

YOUR LEGAL PROTECTION IS OUR RESPONSIBILITY

Every worker placed by Oakwell is covered by our full compliance framework. You are protected from day one.

Full EL cover from the moment any worker is placed at your site. Certificates available on request.

Public Liability Insurance

Minimum £2m PLI cover. Evidence provided as part of our client onboarding pack.

Right-to-Work Checks

Every worker's right to work in the UK is checked and documented before their first shift.

All workers paid at or above National Living Wage. Full PAYE, NI, and pension obligations met.
