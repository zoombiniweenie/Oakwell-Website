Compliance & Protection

YOUR LEGAL PROTECTION IS OUR RESPONSIBILITY

Every worker placed by Oakwell is covered by our full compliance framework. You are fully protected from day one.

🛡️

Employers Liability Insurance

Full EL cover from the moment any worker is placed at your site. Certificates available on request.

🏛️

Public Liability Insurance

Minimum £2m PLI cover. Evidence provided as part of our client onboarding pack.

📑

Right-to-Work Checks

Every worker's right to work in the UK is checked and documented before their first shift, in line with the Immigration, Asylum and Nationality Act 2006.

⚖️

AWR Compliance

We track every worker's qualifying weeks and manage AWR entitlements proactively — you'll never be caught out by a week-12 claim you weren't expecting.

💷

NMW & PAYE Compliant

All workers paid at or above National Living Wage. Full PAYE, NI and pension obligations met. We carry the employer cost burden — not you.

🔒

UK GDPR & Data Protection

ICO registered. Worker and client data handled in accordance with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.
