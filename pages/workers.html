Pay Explained

HOW YOUR PAY WORKS

No jargon, no small print. Here is exactly how your pay is calculated and when you get it.

YOUR HOURLY RATE

Set before your assignment starts. Always at or above the National Living Wage. Never a surprise.

+ HOLIDAY PAY (12.07%)

Holiday pay is added to every payslip as a separate line. You can see exactly what you are owed.

PAID WEEKLY

Based on your signed timesheet. Direct to your bank account every week.

What gets deducted? Only what is required by law — Income Tax (PAYE) and National Insurance. That is it. We never charge you any fees, and we never deduct anything without putting it clearly on your payslip.
