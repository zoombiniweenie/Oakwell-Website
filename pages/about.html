Who We Are

A FAMILY BUSINESS BUILT ON TRUST

Oakwell Workforce Limited was founded in Stourbridge with one simple belief: that industrial staffing should be built on relationships, not transactions. That workers should be known, not just processed. That clients should have a real person to call, not a queue to join.

Our family has deep roots in the Black Country. This region is known for its people — hard-working, direct, and proud of what they make. We share those values, and they shape everything about how we run this agency.

We started Oakwell because we saw a gap that the national agencies had created: a space where no one knew anyone personally, where workers were numbers and clients were accounts, and where a 6am phone call was handled by an automated system. We built this agency to be the antidote to all of that.

What We Stand For

OUR VALUES

Not a wall of corporate buzzwords. These are the principles we make decisions by.

01
🤝

People First, Always

Every worker is a person with bills, family, and ambitions — not a unit of labour. Every client is a business owner with real problems. We never forget that on either side of the relationship.

02
📞

Availability When It Counts

Anyone can answer the phone at 2pm on a Tuesday. The test is 5:45am on a Monday when a shift has fallen apart. That's when we answer — because that's when our clients actually need us.

03
✅

Honesty Over Sales

We would rather tell a client we can't fill a position than send someone unsuitable. We would rather tell a worker a role isn't right for them than place them in a job they'll walk out of on day two. Honesty builds longer relationships than promises.

04
📍

Rooted in This Region

We're not parachuting in from a head office somewhere else. Stourbridge is home. The Black Country is where we built this business, and the communities it serves are ours. That creates accountability no national agency can replicate.

05
⚖️

Compliance Without Compromise

Every worker right-to-work checked. Every placement fully insured. AWR tracked from day one. PAYE handled properly. These aren't boxes we tick — they're the foundation of how we operate.

06
📈

Growth With Integrity

We want to grow — but not at the expense of what makes us different. Every new client, every new worker added to our books will be handled with the same care we gave to the first. Scale does not mean standards slip.
